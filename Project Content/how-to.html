How to Use the System

Follow these steps to register, sign in, navigate features, and manage reports effectively.

1

Step One

Register your account

  1. Go to Create Account.
  2. Use your UMak email (e.g., your.name@umak.edu.ph).
  3. Click Create Account to receive a verification email.
2

Step Two

Verify your email

  • Open your inbox and click the verification link.
  • If you don't see it, check Spam/Promotions.
  • Use "Resend verification email" if needed.
3

Step Three

Sign in

  • Go to Login and enter your UMak email and password.
  • Admins are routed to the Admin Dashboard automatically.
  • Use "Forgot password?" to reset your password.
4

Step Four

View seats and map

  • See seat availability and table numbers on your dashboard.
  • Use the map to locate quiet zones and collaborative areas.
  • Colors indicate occupancy; refresh for latest status.
5

Step Five

Report noise or issues

  • Open the report form in your dashboard.
  • Select table/location, pick type, and add details.
  • Admins receive alerts and can resolve or follow up.

Tips

  • Use your UMak email to register. Other domains are blocked.
  • If you don't get an email, use the resend button after 2 minutes.
  • Keep your browser open during verification to complete signup smoothly.
